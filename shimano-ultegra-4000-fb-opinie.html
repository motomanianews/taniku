What is Shimano Ultegra 4000 FB?

The Shimano Ultegra 4000 FB is a rodeo-level groupset designed for professional riders and serious enthusiasts. It's an updated version of the popular Ultegra 6800 series, boasting improved ergonomics, increased efficiency, and enhanced performance. The FB designation stands for"Flat Bar," indicating that this gearset is specifically designed for flat bar road bikes.

Features and Specifications

The Shimano Ultegra 4000 FB features a number of innovative technologies that set it apart from its predecessors:

  • New ErgoDynamic design: The gearset's ergonomic design provides a more comfortable riding position, reduced fatigue, and improved control.
  • Improved Shifters: The redesigned shifters feature a more ergonomic shape, increased compatibility with Shimano's internal cable routing system, and improved shifting performance.
  • Enhanced Gear Range: The Ultegra 4000 FB offers a wider range of gears (11-28T) for improved climbing ability and reduced weight.
  • Increased Braking Power: The Ultegra 4000 FB features Shimano's highly-regarded hydraulic disc brakes, providing improved stopping power in wet and dry conditions.
  • Lighter and More Durable: The gearset is built with lightweight materials and features increased durability, ensuring a longer lifespan and reduced maintenance.
